Maze Game

Overview

The Maze Game is an exciting experience in which players navigate their way around the complicated boundries of the maze, all while being bombarded whith constant obstacles and racing against the clock.

How To Play

Players must guide the ball through a randomized maze while dodging falling objects. Players must reach the end of the maze to win.

Rules

You start with 4000 points.

Every boundry hit subtracts X points.

Every falling object hit sutracts 1000 points.

Reach the end of the maze as fast as possible with hitting the least amount of boundries and objects to achieve a high score.
